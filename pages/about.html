ABOUT ICSearch

"Simplifying your journey through
the Physical Science Building"

ICSearch is a web application dedicated to helping students easily find specific rooms and faculty members within the Institute of Computer Science (ICS). By providing a user-friendly interface, ICSearch seeks to give students the quick access to directions to laboratories, lecture rooms, and professors' offices. ICSearch aims to guide students in navigating the ICS building without worrying about losing their way. Similarly, it also seeks to provide students with the ability to contact ICS professors conveniently and timely, ensuring smoother communication and easier access to faculty support.
